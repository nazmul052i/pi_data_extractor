Spike Period

The Spike Period is the length of time (in absolute time units) that consecutive data samples must remain above a variableâ€™s Spike Threshold before IQmodel considers that the higher values do not constitute faults, but rather that the process has transitioned to a different operating level. In other words, the Spike Period in effect determines the number of consecutive Spike Faults that IQmodel will report. IQmodel ensures that the Spike Period is a multiple of the Base Time of the model.
