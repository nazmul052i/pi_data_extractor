Spike Threshold

When a variableâ€™s sample-to-sample value exceeds its Spike Threshold, a Spike Fault is reported (assuming Spike Detection is enabled) and a Spike Period timer is started. If, for the duration of the Spike Period, the absolute value of subsequent samples remains above the value (absolute value of sample + threshold) that generated the fault, samples collected after the Spike Period has elapsed will no longer be marked bad due to Spike Faults (though all samples collected during the Spike Period interval will be marked bad).
