Inferential Sensing

Inferential Sensing is the science of using mathematical models to generate appropriate values for difficult- or costly-to-measure process properties. In an Inferential Sensing application, the value of a critical process variable or product property is inferred from the values of other process variables and their relationships. This yields a model that provides a continuous stream of values by simulating the behavior of a sensor monitoring the variable or property.
