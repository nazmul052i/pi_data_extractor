Out of Date

In the context of building models with IQmodel, Out of Date means that calculations associated with a model step have been performed and results are available for use in subsequent steps; however, one or more parameters associated with the step have been changed since the calculations were performed. When the dialog for the step is closed, a warning will be displayed that indicates that results are possibly inconsistent (unless this warning has been turned off through the Options: dialog).

When the dialog closes, at minimum the box in the Commands window for the immediately following model step will be made available (boxes representing other subsequent steps may also be available, depending upon prior progress in building the current model).
