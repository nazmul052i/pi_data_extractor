Modified Roulette Wheel

The Modified Roulette Wheel method for determining parents is based on the Roulette Wheel method. The modification essentially guarantees the selection of an individual for reproduction if its fitness is greater than 1. For example, if a member has a fitness between 2 and 3, it will be selected as a parent by the Modified Roulette Wheel method at least twice.
