Dead Time

Dead Time is the delay between when the value of an independent model variable changes and when the model dependent variable begins to change in response. A dead time of 0 indicates that the dependent variable responds immediately when the corresponding independent variable changes.

In IQmodel, dead times are specified as times in the form of Hours:Minutes:Seconds (HH:MM:SS). Zero fields are not displayed. For example, :06 means 6 seconds; :01:30 means 1 minute, 30 seconds.
