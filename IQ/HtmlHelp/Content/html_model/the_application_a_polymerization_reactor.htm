The Application: a Polymerization Reactor

Polymer production is one of the most common processes that rely on inferential sensing. Due to impurities in raw materials, side reactions cannot be effectively modeled and first principles-based models are inaccurate. In addition, online measuring technology is often either very expensive or not available for properties such as Melt Index or Density, thus process management must rely on laboratory measurements obtained at irregular sampling intervals.
