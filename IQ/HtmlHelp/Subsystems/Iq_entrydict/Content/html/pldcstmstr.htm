PLDCSTMSTR

Sample time for a previous lab sample. The sample taken time for a new sample is determined based on the STIMEOPT specification. When a new sample is taken the Lab Data Collection (LDC) module shift the previous samples list down and sets the sample time in the top element of the list equal to the new (latest) sample time. The status of the new sample is either "Waiting" or "In Lab" depending on the NUMWAIT entry. When NUMWAIT is greater than zero (> 0), there is one sample with the "Waiting" status whose sample time matches the NEWLABSTMSTR for new lab data entry. Any additional samples which are also not "Stored" in the LDC_history_file will have the "In Lab" status.
