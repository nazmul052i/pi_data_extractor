PREDBIASX

Transformed prediction bias used in the online prediction calculation in conjunction with transforms. The transformed prediction bias is added to the UPRX and UAZX values to obtain BPRX and BAZX, respectively. The prediction bias is calculated by filtering the ABIASNEW or LBIASNEW values depending on the update source specified in UPDSRC.
