STKNMON

Month specification for new sample taken time. This entry is used when the sample time option STIMEOPT is 4 (Integer) or 5 (Integer + Flag). The sample time interpreted by the Aspen IQ application is displayed in the STKTMSTROUT entry.

Applies to:  

Lab Data Collection: General variables

Data Type:  

Integer

Access:  

Read Only

Range:  

1 to 12

Default:  

1 (Required Entry)

Keywords:  

LOCAL READ CONSTANT

Related Topics:  

STIMEOPT, STKNDAY, STKNHOUR, STKNMIN, STKNSEC, STKNYEAR, STKTMSTROUT
