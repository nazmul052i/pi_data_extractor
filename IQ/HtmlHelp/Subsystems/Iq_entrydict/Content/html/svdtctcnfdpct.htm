SVDTCTCNFDPCT

SVDTCTCNFDPCT is the confidence percentage used for calculating the detection index threshold. This calculation is based on confidence limits for the known statistical distribution of the detection index under the hypothesis that no fault is present. SVDTCTCNFDPCT is typically 95% or 99%. Increasing SVDTCTCNFDPCT decreases the probability of false detection, but also decreases the number of detected faults.
